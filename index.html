Safety & Test Plan

process
  • HV safety: insulation checks, proper fusing, correct cable rating, strain relief, labeling.
  • Interlocks: HVIL loop, service disconnect, e-stop behavior, “ready-to-drive” indicators.
  • Precharge validation: verify capacitor charge profile and contactor timing.
  • Functional tests: throttle plausibility, brake switch, direction logic, regen limits.
  • Failure modes: contactor weld detection, overcurrent, overtemp, BMS fault handling.

Make sure your implementation follows applicable electrical safety practices and any local inspection rules.
