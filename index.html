Electric Motor Project

Project focused on electric motor integration and my task: design and mounting of a hall sensor bracket for rotor position and RPM measurement. The work was done as part of a course where ~30 students aimed to convert a Volvo XC40 from an internal combustion engine (ICE) drivetrain to electric drive using a student-built powertrain. The project was split into multiple subteams with clear responsibilities, and my contribution was in the motor team—delivering a mechanically stable solution that provided a reliable hall sensor signal during testing and operation.

BLDC Hall Sensors SolidWorks 3D Printing

My contribution: Hall sensor mount

  • Mechanical mounting to the stator
  • Correct spacing to the rotor magnets
  • Cable routing and vibration robustness

The design was iterated after testing to improve signal strength and fit. The final version provided stable detection during operation.
